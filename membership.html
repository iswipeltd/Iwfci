  • How do I apply for membership?

    To become a member, download our application form, fill it out completely, and send it to Iwfcinigeria@gmail.com. Our team will review your application and get back to you shortly.

    Download Application Form

  • What documents do I need to submit?

    You'll need to complete our membership application form with your personal information, professional background, and reasons for joining. Additional supporting documents may be requested during the review process.

  • How long does the review process take?

    Once you submit your completed application form to Iwfcinigeria@gmail.com, our review committee will assess your application within 7-14 business days. You will receive an email notification regarding the status of your application.

  • What are the membership requirements?

    Membership requirements include completion of the application form, alignment with our organization's mission and values, and approval by our membership committee. Specific criteria are detailed in the application form.

  • Is there a membership fee?

    Information about membership fees and payment options is included in the application form. Please review the form carefully for complete details about membership costs and benefits.

  • What happens after I'm approved?

    Once your application is approved, you'll receive a welcome email with instructions on accessing member benefits, upcoming events, and how to get involved with our organization's initiatives and programs.
