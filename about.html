Our Focus

Our Goals

  • • Gain recognition and access to international agencies supporting women.
  • • Provide products, services and programs that assist women in business.
  • • Represent women with entrepreneurial drive across commerce and industry.
  • • Create enabling environments for women to contribute and lead.
  • • Advocate with government and institutions on issues relevant to women in business and trade.
About IWFCI

Background & History

IWFCI is recognised as the world’s first International Women’s Chamber of Commerce. Founded in 1992 in Melbourne by Diana Abruzzi, IWFCI was established to support women entering business and building international trade. Its global secretariat remains in Melbourne, coordinating national chapters and international programs.

As society and technology rapidly evolve, IWFCI continues to strengthen infrastructure and support for women — acknowledging their growing impact across commerce, community and government.

Our Mandate

What We Do

  • • Provide training and mentoring to strengthen women‑led enterprises.
  • • Build networking platforms and peer communities across industries.
  • • Foster new business through global summits and trade delegations.
  • • Promote women in business and trade as decision‑makers and leaders.
  • • Advance international cooperation while shaping national economic participation.
