Event Description

Join us for the Global Women's Trade Summit, a premier international gathering of women entrepreneurs, business leaders, and industry experts from around the world. This transformative two-day event in Melbourne, Australia, brings together visionary women leading global trade through innovation and cooperation to network, collaborate, and explore opportunities in global trade and commerce.

The summit features inspiring keynote sessions, strategic business workshops, trade facilitation programs, and exclusive networking opportunities designed to empower women in business and foster meaningful connections across borders.
