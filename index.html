Empowering Women in Business

IWFCI Nigeria

IWFCI is a non-profit, non-government, member-based organisation. It was established with the purpose of supporting women entering business and developing trade across the globe. Today, IWFCI remains first and foremost an empowering opportunity for women to meet and engage with like-minded entrepreneurs to grow their businesses in a supportive network.

Learn More
What We Offer

Our Services

Empower your business growth on the global stage with the International Women's Federation of Commerce and Industry South Africa (IWFCI). Our focus is on guiding business through transformative journeys in global trade. Through the delivery of a powerful global chapter network, inspiring business summits, social networking events, confidential support forums and uplifting educational programs; bringing ambitious business women together from around the world to grow and prosper.

Networking Opportunities

Forge meaningful connections with like-minded women leaders, entrepreneurs, and professionals through exclusive networking events, conferences, and forums organized by the International Women's Federation of Commerce and Industry Nigeria. Build a powerful network that opens doors to collaboration, mentorship, and valuable business relationships.

Trade Facilitation Programs

Access specialised trade initiatives designed to empower women in business. Our federation provides support in navigating international trade, offering resources, guidance, and platforms to enhance the visibility and success of women-owned enterprises on the global stage

Professional Development

Elevate your skills and knowledge through a range of workshops and forums tailored to the needs of women in commerce and industry. From leadership development to business strategy, our offerings empower you to excel in your professional journey.

Advocacy and Representation

Benefit from a strong advocate for women in commerce and industry. Our federation actively engages in advocacy efforts, ensuring that the voices and concerns of women entrepreneurs are heard at local, national, and international levels. We work to create an enabling environment for women-led businesses

Grow Your Business Initiatives

Leverage resources and programs aimed at accelerating the growth of your business. Whether through mentorship programs, market expansion strategies, or access to innovation hubs, the International Women's Federation of Commerce and Industry Nigeria is dedicated to supporting the sustained growth and success of women entrepreneurs.

Market Expansion Services

Our vast network provides valuable connections, opening doors to potential collaborators, investors, and clients, further amplifying your market presence. With our finger on the pulse of emerging trends, we equip you with foresight, enabling proactive decision-making that ensures not only growth but sustainable success in dynamic and evolving markets.
